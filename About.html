Our Story

Every year, millions of students search for reliable educational resources, exam guidance, college information, and career advice. Unfortunately, this information is often scattered across many different platforms.

Zerax was created with one simple vision— to bring everything into one place. Our platform aims to make learning easier, career planning smarter, and educational guidance more accessible for every student.
